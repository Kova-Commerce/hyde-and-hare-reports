What worked in primary copy

Five patterns repeat across statistically meaningful winners.

  1. Question hook + bullet/list body. Ad 215 (seat pad, ROAS 8.33) and Ad 63 (rugs, ROAS 5.46) both lead with a question and use a vertical list of scenarios. List format is the most repeatable winning structure.
  2. Short-sentence sensory close. "Beautifully padded. Impossibly soft." (Ad 210, ROAS 8.29) and "Softness you notice. Warmth you feel." (Ad 205, ROAS 10.65). Three short fragments beat one full sentence.
  3. 5-star review embed. Ads 191, 172, 205 all carry review quotes with star markers. Lifts conversion across cold and warm.
  4. Origin / craft anchor. "Real New Zealand sheepskin, handcrafted in England" / "Tanned in small sustainable batches" / "Arctic regions of Scandinavia." Always present in winners.
  5. Value carried in the headline. Ad 1 used "Free Worldwide Delivery" as the headline and hit ROAS 9.54. The headline is doing real work, not just the body.

Seat Pad GWP Learnings

Same £150 threshold, same audience, same lookalike sheepskin product. The closest analog we have. Two formulas dominated.

Ad 210 (£2.8k total spend, ROAS 4-8): "Ever noticed how the smallest details tie a whole room together?" + product description + "Beautifully padded. Impossibly soft."

Ad 215 (£1.1k total spend, ROAS 5-8): "Where do you find your quiet moments at home?" + bulleted list + "Sheepskin seat pads bring natural softness to any of them."

Key observation: Both winning seat pad ads sold the product, not the GWP offer. The £150+ sat in landing page / cart messaging, not ad copy. We should test both directions for cushions.

Notes & Guardrails

  • No em dashes. Consistent with brand voice rule.
  • "Complimentary" is the only word used to express the offer. Do not substitute "free", "on us", or "on the house".
  • No "cozy". Spring and summer framing leans on softness, texture, natural tones, slow mornings, lingering, open windows.
  • Reviews in V4 and V12 are verified Stamped reviews from cushion product pages on hydeandhare.com. Do not edit or paraphrase. Attribution required.
  • £150 threshold is disclosed in every offer-led version (Meta policy requires this).
  • Consider "Free worldwide delivery" tag on remarketing variants. That anchor lifted Ad 1 to ROAS 9.54.
